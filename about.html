Origin Story

How Pixel Rebels started

We're three friends — a designer, a developer, and a creative specialist — who kept seeing the same problem: small businesses either couldn't afford a proper website, or they'd pay a big agency and end up with something generic that didn't represent them.

So we built Pixel Rebels. A small, focused studio where every project gets real attention, not just a slot in a production line. We handle design, development, branding, and SEO — everything you need to look professional online and start getting leads.

We're based in London, we work with businesses across the UK, and we keep things simple: clear pricing, honest timelines, and a website you're actually proud of.
