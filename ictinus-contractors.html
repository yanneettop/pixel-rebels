Client Project — Trades & Construction

Ictinus Contractors

A full website and brand build for a London-based decorating and refurbishment contractor — designed to turn their strong offline reputation into a professional online presence that generates leads.

The Brief

What they needed

Ictinus Contractors had 12+ years of experience, a 9.97/10 Checkatrade rating, 60+ verified reviews, and a growing reputation across East London — but no website. Their leads came through platforms like Checkatrade and MyBuilder, which meant they were competing on those platforms' terms and paying for every enquiry. They needed their own site: something that established credibility independently, showcased their work, and gave potential clients a direct way to get in touch.

What We Delivered

A lead-generating contractor website

Brand positioning

Positioned Ictinus as a premium, reliable contractor, not just another painter, with messaging built around trust, quality standards, and verified results.

Multi-page website

Home, About, Services with 7 service categories, Portfolio with 6 projects and gallery, Contact with quote form, and Testimonials.

Checkatrade & MyBuilder integration

Live review scores, 9.97/10 and 4.9/5, displayed prominently across the site and linked to verified profiles.

Copywriting

All website copy crafted to speak to homeowners, landlords, letting agents, and commercial clients, each with their own relevant messaging.

Portfolio with photo galleries

A project-by-project showcase with location, service type, and expandable image galleries.

SEO & local search

Optimised for searches like "decorator London", "refurbishment East London", and relevant service-specific keywords.

Conversion strategy

Multiple CTA placements, strong trust signals, and a direct quote flow built to turn visitors into enquiries.
