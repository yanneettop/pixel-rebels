Client Project — Hospitality

Coffee Matters London

A full brand and website build for an independent specialty café on Brick Lane — from zero online presence to a site that drives foot traffic, event bookings, and 200+ Google reviews.

The Brief

What they needed

Coffee Matters is a specialty café on Brick Lane, London, founded by three Greek friends. They had a loyal local following but no website — their online presence was limited to an Instagram page and a Google Maps listing. They needed a site that captured the warmth and character of the café, showcased their menu and events, and gave them credibility online to attract new visitors and bookings.

What We Delivered

A complete digital identity

Brand direction & visual identity

Refined their brand around the Mediterranean-meets-Brick-Lane personality that makes them distinctive.

Full website design & development

A single-page site with smooth scroll navigation, hero imagery, menu section, gallery, about story, and contact/location information.

Copywriting

All website copy written to reflect their voice: warm, inviting, and proudly Greek.

Google Reviews integration

A live reviews section pulling their 4.8-star rating and latest reviews directly onto the site, building instant trust with new visitors.

SEO foundation

Optimised for local searches like "coffee shop Brick Lane" and "specialty café East London".

Instagram integration

Connecting their active social presence to the website for ongoing content.

Event venue promotion

A dedicated section to drive private event enquiries, opening a new revenue stream.
