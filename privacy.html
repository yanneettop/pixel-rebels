Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Pixel Rebels ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you contact us or request a quote:

  • Name and email address
  • Business name and website URL
  • Project details you share with us
  • Basic usage data (pages visited, time on site) via analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and provide quotes
  • Deliver the services you've requested
  • Send project updates and relevant communications
  • Improve our website and services

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with trusted service providers (e.g. email tools) who assist us in operating our business, under strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time without affecting your ability to use the site.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@pixelrebels.space.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
